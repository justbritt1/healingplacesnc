About Us

Healing Places is a non-profit organization dedicated to creating safe spaces for healing and growth. We focus on providing support, resources, and services to individuals and communities in need. Our mission is to foster emotional, mental, and physical well-being for all.

Our Programs

Community Outreach

We offer outreach services to underserved communities, providing access to resources and educational workshops.

Counseling & Support

Our counseling services are designed to provide emotional support to individuals in need, with a focus on trauma recovery.

Wellness Workshops

We offer wellness workshops and activities to promote mental and physical health, including yoga, mindfulness, and art therapy.
